Nomination Process

Pathway to Prestige

Transparent and rigorous evaluation to honour the brightest minds in biotechnology.

Step 1: Submission

Complete the online nomination form and upload supporting documents by 15 December 2024.

Step 2: Screening

Expert committee evaluates eligibility and shortlists top candidates for jury review.

Step 3: Jury Deliberation

Jury members assess innovation, impact, scalability, and leadership to select finalists.

Step 4: Awards Night

Winners are honoured on 20 February 2025 in New Delhi amidst industry leaders and policy makers.
